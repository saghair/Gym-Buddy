Training programs

Structured plans you can follow for several weeks. Pick one and stick to it.

4-Week Beginner Full Body

4 weeks

Three full-body sessions per week, focusing on basic movements, perfect for new lifters or people coming back after a break.

Day Focus
Day 1Full body A (squats, push, row)
Day 2Light cardio + core
Day 3Full body B (hinge, push, pull)
Day 4Rest / walk
Day 5Full body A
Day 6–7Rest / easy activity
Repeat this weekly schedule for 4 weeks, slowly adding reps or weight.

6-Week Push / Pull / Legs

Intermediate

Classic split where each day focuses on a specific group of muscles. Great if you like being in the gym 4–5 times per week.

Day Focus
Day 1Push (chest, shoulders, triceps)
Day 2Pull (back, biceps)
Day 3Legs + core
Day 4Rest / light cardio
Day 5Push / Pull mix
Day 6Legs + conditioning
Day 7Rest
Follow for 6 weeks, focus on good form and small weekly progress.
